Is Lidl Ethical?

Lidl, a German discount supermarket chain, has been accused of extensively spying on employees, recording personal details like menstrual cycles, relationships and finances [1], as well as shutting down stores where employees unionise [2]. The company has also faced allegations of animal cruelty [3], using unethical killing methods on chickens, operating in inhumane conditions, and deceiving consumers about severing ties with an abusive supplier farm.

Lidl is owned by schwarz-gruppe.
