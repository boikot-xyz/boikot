Is Wagamama Ethical?

Wagamama is a British restaurant chain that has underpaid staff and failed to pay the National Minimum Wage [1], and has been accused of maintaining a policy that discourages sick employees from taking time off [2]. The company has also faced criticism for requiring staff to cover the cost of uniforms, which was considered an infringement on workers' rights [1].

Wagamama is owned by apollo-global-management.
